# Mission and Vision

## Mission
Atlas Systems empowers Greek and European enterprises to adopt modern cloud-native architectures with production-grade reliability, by combining deep engineering expertise with long-term partnerships rather than one-off project delivery.

## Vision
To be the most trusted Greek engineering partner for organisations migrating mission-critical workloads to the cloud — a peer-level technical voice in our clients' planning conversations, not a faceless vendor.

## Core Values
1. **Engineering integrity** — we recommend the technology that is right for the client, not the technology that maximises billable hours.
2. **Long-term thinking** — we measure success in years of relationship, not quarters of revenue.
3. **Operational excellence** — every system we deliver must be observable, recoverable, and safe to evolve.
4. **Local roots, European reach** — Greek-based, EU-oriented; we contribute to the Greek tech ecosystem while serving clients beyond our borders.
5. **Knowledge sharing** — internal tech talks, open-source contributions, and a €1,200 per-employee annual training budget are non-negotiable.

## What We Do Not Do
- Body-shopping (placing engineers without architectural responsibility).
- Vendor-locked recommendations.
- Throwaway prototypes presented as production-ready systems.
