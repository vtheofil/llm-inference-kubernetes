# Atlas Systems

Atlas Systems is a Greek technology consulting company founded in **2014** and headquartered in **Athens, Greece**. The company specialises in cloud-native software engineering, distributed systems, and AI/data platform consulting for enterprise clients across Greece and the European Union.

## At a Glance
- **Founded**: 2014
- **Headquarters**: Athens, Greece
- **Total employees**: approximately 200
- **Active projects**: 50+
- **Offices**: 3 (Athens HQ, Thessaloniki, Patras) plus a fully remote workforce
- **CEO**: Maria Voulgari
- **Legal form**: Private limited company (Greek Ι.Κ.Ε.)
- **Annual revenue**: ~€18 million (2024)

## Service Areas
Atlas Systems operates four primary practice areas:
1. **Software Engineering** — custom backend, frontend, and full-stack development
2. **DevOps & SRE** — cloud infrastructure, Kubernetes, observability, CI/CD
3. **Data & AI** — data engineering, machine learning, MLOps platforms
4. **IT Managed Services** — corporate IT, support, security operations

## Industries Served
Atlas works primarily with banking, transportation, healthcare, retail, energy, and public-sector clients. Representative engagements include long-running contracts with Bank of Greece, Aegean Airlines, MedicalCorp Greece, RetailChain Hellas, EnergyOne, and the Government of Greece.

## Why "Atlas"
The name references the Titan who held up the celestial sphere — chosen to evoke the company's role as a long-term infrastructure partner to its clients.
