# Partnerships

Atlas Systems maintains formal technology and strategic partnerships that enable us to deliver work on partner platforms at competitive licensing and support terms.

## Cloud Provider Partnerships
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** — Advanced Tier Services Partner.
- **Microsoft Azure** — Gold Cloud Solution Provider.
- **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)** — Premier Partner.

These tiers entitle Atlas to dedicated partner managers, joint go-to-market support, and access to free credits used in client proofs-of-concept.

## Technology Vendor Partnerships
- **Snowflake** — Implementation Partner.
- **MongoDB** — Solutions Partner.
- **HashiCorp** — Services Partner (Terraform, Vault).
- **GitLab** — Premium Partner.
- **Confluent** — Partner (Apache Kafka).

## Academic Partnerships
- **University of Patras** — joint R&D programme on edge-computing platforms, anchored at the Patras office.
- **National Technical University of Athens (NTUA)** — informal collaboration on cloud-systems research; Atlas sponsors selected student theses.
- **University of Thessaly** — collaboration with the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications on distributed-systems research.

## Community Engagement
- **Athens Cloud-Native Meetup** — Atlas Systems is a founding sponsor and regularly hosts the meetup at the Athens HQ.
- **Greek DevOps Days** — annual conference sponsor.
- **Internal open-source contributions** — engineers are encouraged to contribute to open-source projects on company time, subject to manager approval.

## How to Engage Atlas Partnerships
Prospective partners and academic institutions can reach out to **partnerships@atlas.com**. Inbound requests are reviewed monthly by the COO together with the relevant practice director.
