# Office Locations

Atlas Systems operates three physical offices in Greece plus a fully supported remote workforce.

## Athens HQ
- **Address**: 142 Kifisias Avenue, 11526 Athens, Greece
- **Role**: Corporate headquarters and primary engineering hub.
- **Hosts**: Executive office, the largest share of the Engineering and Data & AI departments, the Finance and Legal teams.
- **Workspace**: 4 floors, ~120 desks, multiple meeting rooms, an internal demo lab.

## Thessaloniki Office
- **Address**: 18 Tsimiski Street, 54624 Thessaloniki, Greece
- **Role**: Northern Greece delivery centre.
- **Hosts**: A balanced mix of Engineering, DevOps, and Sales staff serving clients in northern Greece and the Balkans.
- **Workspace**: 2 floors, ~50 desks.

## Patras Office
- **Address**: 24 Riga Feraiou Street, 26221 Patras, Greece
- **Role**: Smaller satellite office focused on R&D collaboration with the University of Patras.
- **Hosts**: A smaller team of engineers and the company's research collaborators.
- **Workspace**: 1 floor, ~20 desks.

## Remote
- **Role**: Atlas Systems supports fully remote employment for roles where physical presence is not required.
- **Requirements**: Remote employees must be based in the EEA and must visit their assigned office at least once per quarter (travel reimbursed per the Travel Policy).

## Office Operating Hours
All three physical offices are open Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 19:00 local time. Access outside these hours requires a security badge and is logged.
