# Leadership Team

Atlas Systems is led by **CEO Maria Voulgari**, supported by an executive team representing the company's primary functions. Department directors report directly to the CEO.

## Executive Office
- **Maria Voulgari** — Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Founder of Atlas Systems (2014).

## Direct Reports to the CEO
Each department is headed by its director or equivalent C-level executive, all of whom report to the CEO:

- **Engineering Director** — leads the largest department; responsible for delivery quality across all customer-facing software projects.
- **DevOps / Infrastructure Manager** — leads the DevOps practice; owns the internal Kubernetes platform and the production-environment SLAs.
- **Data & AI Director** — leads the Data & AI practice; responsible for the company's ML platform and data engineering roadmap.
- **IT Support Manager** — leads the internal IT function; corporate helpdesk, endpoint management, identity.
- **HR Director** — leads Human Resources.
- **CFO (Chief Financial Officer)** — leads Finance.
- **Sales Director** — leads sales and account management.
- **CMO (Chief Marketing Officer)** — leads Marketing.
- **COO (Chief Operating Officer)** — leads Operations and cross-functional programmes.
- **General Counsel** — leads the Legal function.

The specific individuals holding each position are listed in the per-department records.

## Board of Directors
The Atlas Systems Board includes the CEO plus three independent directors with backgrounds in Greek industry, EU public-sector consulting, and academic research. Board meetings are held quarterly.
