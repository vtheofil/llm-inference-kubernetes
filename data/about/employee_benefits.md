# Employee Benefits

Atlas Systems offers a competitive benefits package on top of base salary. Benefits apply to all full-time employees from day one.

## Health & Wellbeing
- **Private health insurance** — covering employee, spouse, and dependent children.
- **Annual health check-up** — provided at no cost via partner clinics in Athens, Thessaloniki, and Patras.
- **Mental-health support** — confidential access to licensed therapists via an external provider, up to 8 sessions per year.

## Learning & Development
- **Annual training budget**: **€1,200 per employee** for courses, conferences, books, or certifications.
- **Certification exam fees** covered by Atlas for company-relevant certifications (AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, etc.).
- **Internal tech talks** — bi-weekly, contributing to the training budget when delivered.
- **Conference travel** — additional budget for one industry conference per year, by department director approval.

## Working Arrangements
- **Hybrid by default** — up to 3 days remote per week with manager approval (per the Remote Work Policy).
- **Full remote** — available for selected roles; remote employees must visit their assigned office at least once per quarter.
- **Flexible hours** — core hours are 10:00–16:00 local time.

## Leave (summary; full details in the Annual Leave Policy)
- **25 days** annual leave per year for full-time employees.
- **+3 days** annual leave after 5 years of service (total 28 days).
- **10 days** sick leave per year.
- **17 weeks** maternity leave; **4 weeks** paternity / partner leave.

## Other Perks
- **Equipment refresh** — laptops are refreshed every 3 years; choice between MacBook Pro and high-spec Linux/Windows machines.
- **Office snacks and coffee** at all three physical offices.
- **Team retreats** — annual two-day company-wide retreat in a Greek seaside location.
- **Friday social hour** at the Athens HQ.

## What Atlas Does Not Offer
- Equity / stock options (Atlas is a private limited company; profit-sharing is not standard).
- Company car (transport is reimbursed via the Travel Policy when business-related).
