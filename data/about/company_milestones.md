# Company Milestones

Selected milestones in the history of Atlas Systems since founding.

## 2014 — Founded
Atlas Systems is founded by **Maria Voulgari** in Athens. Initial team of 6 engineers focused on bespoke Java backend development for two early clients.

## 2016 — First managed-services contract
Signed a multi-year managed-services contract with a major Greek bank — the engagement that would later evolve into the long-running Bank of Greece partnership.

## 2017 — Thessaloniki office opens
Second office opens in central Thessaloniki to serve clients in northern Greece. Headcount reaches ~30.

## 2018 — DevOps practice formalised
Atlas Systems formally launches its DevOps & SRE practice as a standalone department. First Kubernetes-based production engagement delivered.

## 2019 — Data & AI practice formalised
Data & AI is established as a fourth practice area. First data-warehouse modernisation projects begin.

## 2020 — Pandemic adaptation
Transition to predominantly remote work during the COVID-19 pandemic; remote-friendly hiring policy formalised. Headcount reaches ~80.

## 2021 — Patras office opens
Third Greek office opens in Patras, anchored by an R&D collaboration with the University of Patras.

## 2022 — EU expansion
First EU clients outside Greece signed. Atlas begins cross-border engagements through TechVentures EU.

## 2023 — Headcount milestone
Atlas Systems crosses 150 employees. The Engineering department grows to multiple sub-teams.

## 2024 — Tenth anniversary
Atlas Systems celebrates 10 years. Annual revenue reaches approximately €18 million. Headcount approaches 200.

## 2025 — Current
Active investment in internal LLM/RAG platforms (Project Atlas, Project Phoenix) for both internal use and as commercial offerings. Continued growth across all practices.
