# Annual Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to **25 days** of annual leave per year. After **5 years** of service, employees receive an additional **3 days** per year, for a total of 28 days. Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

## Effective Date
January 1, 2025
