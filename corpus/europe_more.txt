SOURCE: Europe Travel Guide — Portugal, Netherlands, Germany, Czech Republic, Croatia, Austria

=== PORTUGAL ===

LISBON (LISBOA):
Average daily costs: Budget 50-65 EUR | Mid-range 100-150 EUR

Top attractions:
- Belém Tower: 6 EUR
- Jerónimos Monastery: 10 EUR
- National Tile Museum (Azulejos): 5 EUR
- São Jorge Castle: 10 EUR
- LX Factory (Sunday market): FREE
- Sintra day trip (30 min train, 3 EUR): Pena Palace 14 EUR | Moorish Castle 8 EUR

Getting around:
- Metro single: 1.60 EUR | 24h pass: 6.80 EUR
- Historic tram 28: 3 EUR (or use metro to avoid crowds)
- Train Lisbon → Sintra: 2.35 EUR (40 min)

Food: Pastel de nata (custard tart): 1.20 EUR | Bacalhau meal: 10-15 EUR | Bifanas (pork sandwich): 3-4 EUR
Recommended stay: 3-4 days

PORTO:
Average daily costs: Budget 40-55 EUR | Mid-range 80-120 EUR
- Ribeira (riverside UNESCO area): FREE
- Livraria Lello bookshop: 8 EUR
- Dom Luís I Bridge walk: FREE
- Wine tasting at Port wine cellars (Vila Nova de Gaia): 10-20 EUR
- São Bento station (azulejo tiles): FREE

Recommended stay: 2-3 days
Train Lisbon → Porto: 3h, 25-40 EUR

Flights from Greece: Athens to Lisbon 80-160 EUR return

=== NETHERLANDS ===

AMSTERDAM:
Average daily costs: Budget 70-90 EUR | Mid-range 140-200 EUR

Top attractions:
- Rijksmuseum (Dutch masters): 22.50 EUR
- Van Gogh Museum: 22 EUR (book online — sells out weeks ahead)
- Anne Frank House: 16 EUR (book online 2+ months ahead)
- Heineken Experience: 23 EUR
- Canal cruise: 12-18 EUR
- Vondelpark: FREE
- Jordaan neighbourhood walk: FREE

Getting around:
- GVB 24h pass: 9 EUR | 72h pass: 19 EUR
- Renting a bike: 10-15 EUR/day (most authentic Amsterdam experience)
- Train from Schiphol airport: 5.50 EUR (20 min)

Food: Stroopwafel: 1 EUR | Herring (haring): 4-5 EUR | Dutch cheese at market: 3-6 EUR | Kroket: 2-3 EUR

Recommended stay: 3 days
Flights from Greece: Athens to Amsterdam 70-150 EUR return

=== GERMANY ===

BERLIN:
Average daily costs: Budget 55-70 EUR | Mid-range 110-160 EUR

Top attractions (many are FREE):
- Brandenburg Gate: FREE
- Holocaust Memorial: FREE
- East Side Gallery (Berlin Wall murals): FREE
- Checkpoint Charlie: FREE (museum 14.50 EUR)
- Pergamon Museum: 21 EUR (or museum island day pass 29 EUR)
- Berlin TV Tower (Fernsehturm): 22.50 EUR
- Tiergarten park: FREE

Food: Currywurst: 3-4 EUR | Döner kebab: 4-6 EUR | Pretzel: 1-2 EUR | Beer at biergarten: 3-5 EUR

Recommended stay: 3-4 days

MUNICH:
Average daily costs: Budget 65-80 EUR | Mid-range 130-190 EUR
- English Garden (huge park): FREE
- BMW Museum: 10 EUR
- Nymphenburg Palace: 8 EUR
- Hofbräuhaus beer hall: FREE to enter (beer 10-12 EUR/litre)
- Oktoberfest (September-October): Entry FREE, beer 13-14 EUR/Maß (1 litre)

Day trip: Neuschwanstein Castle (basis of Disney's castle): 15 EUR + 30 EUR return train/bus from Munich

Recommended stay: 2-3 days
Flights from Greece: Athens to Berlin/Munich 60-140 EUR return

=== CZECH REPUBLIC ===

PRAGUE:
Average daily costs: Budget 40-55 EUR | Mid-range 80-120 EUR (great value!)

Top attractions:
- Prague Castle complex: 250 CZK (≈10 EUR)
- Charles Bridge: FREE
- Old Town Square + Astronomical Clock: FREE
- Jewish Quarter (Josefov): 350 CZK (≈14 EUR)
- Wenceslas Square: FREE

Getting around:
- Metro/tram 24h pass: 120 CZK (≈5 EUR)

Food: Svíčková (beef + cream sauce): 200-300 CZK (≈8-12 EUR) | Beer (0.5L): 40-60 CZK (≈2 EUR!) | Trdelník pastry: 70-100 CZK

Prague is one of Europe's best value cities — great food and beer at very low prices.
Recommended stay: 3 days
Flights from Greece: Athens to Prague 60-120 EUR return

=== CROATIA ===

DUBROVNIK:
Average daily costs: Budget 65-80 EUR | Mid-range 130-200 EUR

Top attractions:
- Old Town City Walls walk: 35 EUR
- Old Town (UNESCO): FREE to walk
- Lokrum island boat + nature: 25 EUR
- Game of Thrones locations: FREE to find

SPLIT:
Average daily costs: Budget 50-65 EUR | Mid-range 100-150 EUR
- Diocletian's Palace (lived-in ancient palace): FREE to walk
- Hvar island day trip: 15-20 EUR ferry return

Recommended: Split 2 days + Dubrovnik 2 days
Flights from Greece: Athens to Dubrovnik/Split 50-100 EUR return

=== AUSTRIA ===

VIENNA (WIEN):
Average daily costs: Budget 60-80 EUR | Mid-range 120-180 EUR

Top attractions:
- Schönbrunn Palace: 16 EUR (Grand Tour)
- Belvedere Palace (Klimt's Kiss): 18 EUR
- Vienna State Opera tour: 14 EUR | Performance: 10-200 EUR
- St. Stephen's Cathedral: FREE (tower 6 EUR)
- Kunsthistorisches Museum: 21 EUR
- Naschmarkt (food market): FREE

Food: Wiener Schnitzel: 15-25 EUR | Sacher Torte at Hotel Sacher: 9 EUR | Apfelstrudel: 5-7 EUR | Coffee house Melange: 4-5 EUR

Recommended stay: 3 days
Flights from Greece: Athens to Vienna 60-130 EUR return
