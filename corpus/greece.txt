=== ATHENS (ΑΘΗΝΑ) ===
Capital of Greece, cradle of Western civilization. Mix of ancient history and vibrant modern city.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 45-65 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 90-140 EUR/day
- Luxury: 200+ EUR/day

Top attractions & entry fees:
- Acropolis (Parthenon, Erechtheion, Propylaea): 20 EUR | Combined ticket with 6 sites: 30 EUR
- Acropolis Museum: 10 EUR (free first Sunday of month Oct-Mar)
- Ancient Agora: 10 EUR (included in combined ticket)
- National Archaeological Museum: 12 EUR
- Temple of Zeus: 6 EUR (combined ticket)
- Panathenaic Stadium: 10 EUR
- Kerameikos: 8 EUR

Getting around Athens:
- Metro/tram/bus single ticket: 1.40 EUR
- 24h transit pass: 4.10 EUR
- 5-day pass: 9 EUR
- Taxi from airport (El. Venizelos): ~38 EUR flat rate (day) | ~54 EUR (night)
- Metro from airport to city: 10.50 EUR (40 min)

Food costs:
- Gyros/souvlaki wrap: 2.50-3.50 EUR
- Greek coffee (frappe): 2-3 EUR
- Taverna meal (moussaka or grilled fish): 10-18 EUR
- Seafood mezedes (3-4 dishes for 2): 30-50 EUR

Best neighbourhoods:
- Monastiraki/Plaka: tourist hub, near Acropolis
- Koukaki: local, quieter, cheaper
- Kolonaki: upscale, boutiques and cafés
- Psiri: nightlife and street art

Recommended stay: 2-3 days

Sample 3-day Athens budget (mid-range, per person):
- Hotel (3 nights x 70 EUR): 210 EUR
- Food (3 days x 30 EUR): 90 EUR
- Attractions (Acropolis + museums): 50 EUR
- Transport: 15 EUR
- TOTAL: ~365 EUR/person

=== SANTORINI ===
The iconic volcanic island with white-washed buildings and stunning caldera views. Most expensive Greek island.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 80-100 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 180-300 EUR/day
- Luxury suites (infinity pool): 400-800+ EUR/night

Top experiences:
- Watching sunset from Oia: FREE (arrive 1-2h early for a spot)
- Akrotiri archaeological site (Minoan ruins): 12 EUR
- Red Beach, White Beach, Perissa Black Beach: FREE
- Caldera boat trip (volcano + hot springs): 25-35 EUR
- Wine tasting at local wineries: 15-30 EUR

Getting around Santorini:
- ATV/quad bike rental: 25-40 EUR/day (most popular)
- Car rental: 40-70 EUR/day
- Local bus (KTEL): 1.80 EUR per journey
- Cable car in Fira: 6 EUR one-way

Best villages: Oia (sunsets), Fira (capital, nightlife), Imerovigli (quieter, views), Pyrgos (traditional)

Ferry from Athens (Piraeus):
- High-speed catamaran: 5h, 60-90 EUR
- Regular ferry: 8-10h, 35-50 EUR

Flight from Athens: 50-100 EUR return (30 min)

Recommended stay: 3-4 days
Best time: May-June, September-October (July-August: peak prices + very crowded)

=== MYKONOS ===
Party island, famous for beaches and nightlife. Expensive.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 100-150 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 200-400 EUR/day

Top experiences:
- Little Venice quarter: FREE
- Windmills: FREE (iconic photo spot)
- Paradise Beach club entry: 10-20 EUR + drinks
- Delos island day trip (UNESCO site): 30-35 EUR (ferry + entry)

Ferry from Athens: 2.5-5h, 40-80 EUR
Recommended stay: 2-3 days (July-August)

=== THESSALONIKI ===
Second-largest city, best food scene in Greece, more affordable.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 35-50 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 70-110 EUR/day

Top attractions (mostly free or cheap):
- White Tower: 4 EUR
- Rotunda: 4 EUR
- Arch of Galerius: FREE
- Archaeological Museum: 8 EUR
- Byzantine churches: FREE
- Ano Poli (upper town, Ottoman-era): FREE

Food highlights (Thessaloniki is famous for food):
- Bougatsa (cream pastry): 2-3 EUR
- Koulouri Thessalonikis: 0.50 EUR
- Taverna meal: 8-14 EUR
- Seafood at the port: 15-25 EUR

Recommended stay: 2 days

=== CRETE ===
Largest Greek island. Mix of history, beaches, and gorges.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 50-70 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 100-160 EUR/day

Top experiences:
- Knossos Palace (Minoan): 15 EUR
- Samaria Gorge hike (Europe's longest): 5 EUR entry + 2 EUR exit ferry
- Elafonisi Beach (pink sand): FREE
- Balos Lagoon: 1.50 EUR + boat 10 EUR
- Heraklion Archaeological Museum: 15 EUR

Ferry from Athens (Piraeus): overnight 9h, 35-60 EUR (cabin +20 EUR)
Recommended stay: 5-7 days (rent a car to explore)

=== GREECE BUDGET PLANNING ===

Athens 3 days + Santorini 3 days (budget per person):
- Athens hotel: 180 EUR
- Santorini hotel: 360 EUR
- Athens food + attractions: 120 EUR
- Santorini food + activities: 200 EUR
- Transport (metro, ferry/flight): 130 EUR
- TOTAL: ~990 EUR/person

Athens 3 days + Thessaloniki 2 days (budget per person):
- Hotels: 250 EUR
- Food + attractions: 150 EUR
- Transport (train Athens-Thessaloniki: 30 EUR): 50 EUR
- TOTAL: ~450 EUR/person

Island hopping (Athens + Mykonos + Santorini, 7 days):
- Budget: 900-1200 EUR/person
- Mid-range: 1500-2500 EUR/person

Best time to visit Greece: May-June, September-October
Peak season (July-August): highest prices, most crowded, very hot (35-40°C)
