=== BARCELONA ===
Vibrant coastal city, capital of Catalonia. Architecture, beaches, food, nightlife.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 55-75 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 110-170 EUR/day
- Luxury: 250+ EUR/day

Top attractions & entry fees:
- Sagrada Família (Gaudí): 26 EUR basic | 36 EUR with towers (MUST book online in advance)
- Park Güell (ticketed area): 10 EUR (book online)
- Casa Batlló: 35 EUR (expensive but stunning)
- Casa Milà (La Pedrera): 25 EUR
- Gothic Quarter (Barri Gòtic): FREE
- Barceloneta Beach: FREE
- MNAC (National Art Museum): 12 EUR
- Picasso Museum: 14 EUR

Getting around Barcelona:
- Metro single: 2.55 EUR
- T-Casual (10 trips): 11.35 EUR
- 24h pass: 10.60 EUR
- Bus from airport (Aerobus): 6.75 EUR | Metro (L9 Sud): 5.15 EUR

Food costs:
- Pan con tomate (café): 2-4 EUR
- Menu del día (3-course lunch, includes wine): 12-18 EUR
- Tapas per dish: 4-12 EUR
- Paella (minimum 2 persons): 15-25 EUR/person
- Beer (cerveza): 2-4 EUR

Best neighbourhoods:
- El Born/Gothic Quarter: history + nightlife
- Gràcia: local vibe, less touristy
- Barceloneta: beach area
- Eixample: Gaudí buildings, LGBTQ+ friendly

Recommended stay: 3-4 days

Sample 3-day Barcelona budget (mid-range, per person):
- Hotel (3 nights x 90 EUR): 270 EUR
- Food (3 days x 40 EUR): 120 EUR
- Attractions (Sagrada + Park Güell + 1 more): 70 EUR
- Transport: 30 EUR
- TOTAL: ~490 EUR/person

=== MADRID ===
Spain's capital. Art, culture, parks, incredible food scene. Less touristy than Barcelona.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 50-65 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 100-150 EUR/day

Top attractions:
- Prado Museum: 15 EUR (free Mon-Sat 6-8pm, Sun 5-7pm)
- Reina Sofía Museum (Guernica): 12 EUR (free Mon + Sat pm + Sun am)
- Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum: 13 EUR
- Royal Palace (Palacio Real): 14 EUR
- Retiro Park: FREE
- Puerta del Sol + Gran Vía: FREE

Food highlights:
- Bocadillo de calamares: 3-4 EUR (Madrid specialty)
- Churros con chocolate: 4-6 EUR
- Mercado de San Miguel tapas: 3-8 EUR per item
- Cocido Madrileño (traditional stew): 15-20 EUR
- Vermouth (vermut) + pintxos bar hop: 15-25 EUR

Recommended stay: 2-3 days

=== SEVILLE (SEVILLA) ===
Most Andalusian city. Flamenco, tapas, orange trees. Very hot in summer.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 40-60 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 80-130 EUR/day

Top attractions:
- Real Alcázar (royal palace): 14.50 EUR (book online)
- Seville Cathedral + Giralda tower: 12 EUR
- Plaza de España: FREE
- Flamenco show: 20-35 EUR
- Triana neighbourhood: FREE

Food: Seville has the best free tapas culture — in many bars, tapas come FREE with drinks.
Beer with free tapa: 2-3 EUR

Recommended stay: 2-3 days

=== GRANADA ===
Home of the Alhambra, one of the world's most visited monuments.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 40-60 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 80-120 EUR/day

Top attractions:
- Alhambra + Generalife + Nasrid Palaces: 19 EUR (book 2-3 months in advance — tickets sell out!)
- Albaicín neighbourhood (UNESCO): FREE (views of Alhambra)
- Cathedral + Royal Chapel: 5 EUR

Like Seville, tapas are FREE with drinks in many bars.

Recommended stay: 2 days

=== SPAIN BUDGET PLANNING ===

Barcelona 4 days (per person, mid-range): 490-600 EUR
Madrid 3 days (per person, mid-range): 380-480 EUR
Seville 2 days + Granada 2 days (per person, budget): 300-400 EUR

Spain 7-day trip (Barcelona → Madrid → Seville), per person:
- Budget: 700-900 EUR
- Mid-range: 1100-1500 EUR

Train travel within Spain:
- Barcelona to Madrid (AVE high-speed): 25-80 EUR (2h40)
- Madrid to Seville (AVE): 25-60 EUR (2h30)
- Madrid to Granada: 20-50 EUR (3h via Antequera)

Flights to Spain from Greece:
- Athens to Barcelona: 50-130 EUR return
- Athens to Madrid: 60-140 EUR return
- Book 6-8 weeks in advance

Best time to visit: March-May, September-November (avoid July-August in Seville/Granada — 40°C+)
