SOURCE: France Travel Guide

=== PARIS ===
The City of Light. Art, fashion, food, romance. One of the world's most visited cities.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 70-90 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 140-220 EUR/day
- Luxury: 350+ EUR/day

Top attractions & entry fees:
- Eiffel Tower: 11 EUR (stairs, 2nd floor) | 19.80 EUR (elevator, 2nd floor) | 29.40 EUR (summit)
- Louvre Museum: 22 EUR (book online to skip queues — free for under 18)
- Musée d'Orsay (Impressionist art): 16 EUR
- Palace of Versailles: 21 EUR (45 min from Paris by RER C)
- Notre-Dame Cathedral: free (currently under restoration, partial access)
- Sainte-Chapelle: 13 EUR
- Centre Pompidou: 15 EUR
- Sacré-Cœur (Montmartre): FREE
- Arc de Triomphe: 13 EUR (great views)
- Rooftop of Galeries Lafayette: FREE

Paris Museum Pass (covers 50+ museums, no queues):
- 2 days: 52 EUR | 4 days: 66 EUR | 6 days: 78 EUR

Getting around Paris:
- Metro/RER single: 2.15 EUR
- Carnet 10 trips: 17.35 EUR (t+ tickets)
- Paris Visite pass (2 days): 20.05 EUR
- CDG airport to city (RER B): 11.80 EUR (50 min)
- Taxi CDG to city: 50-55 EUR (flat rate)

Food costs:
- Croissant (bakery): 1.20-2 EUR
- Coffee (expresso): 2-3 EUR
- Crêpe (street): 3-6 EUR
- Baguette sandwich: 4-6 EUR
- Brasserie plat du jour (lunch): 12-18 EUR
- Sit-down restaurant (2 courses): 25-40 EUR
- Michelin-star restaurant: 80-300 EUR/person

Best neighbourhoods:
- Marais (Le Marais): trendy, Jewish quarter, LGBTQ+
- Montmartre: artistic, bohemian, Sacré-Cœur
- Saint-Germain-des-Prés: literary, chic cafés
- Latin Quarter: student vibe, cheaper
- Champs-Élysées area: central but expensive

Recommended stay: 4-5 days minimum

Sample 4-day Paris budget (mid-range, per person):
- Hotel (4 nights x 110 EUR): 440 EUR
- Food (4 days x 45 EUR): 180 EUR
- Attractions (Eiffel + Louvre + Orsay + Versailles): 90 EUR
- Transport (Metro pass + airport): 50 EUR
- TOTAL: ~760 EUR/person

=== NICE & FRENCH RIVIERA (CÔTE D'AZUR) ===
Glamorous Mediterranean coast. Nice, Cannes, Monaco, Antibes.

Average daily costs (Nice):
- Budget: 65-80 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 130-200 EUR/day

Top experiences:
- Promenade des Anglais (beachfront walk): FREE
- Vieux-Nice (Old Town): FREE
- Castle Hill (Colline du Château): FREE (great panorama)
- Matisse Museum: 10 EUR
- Marc Chagall Museum: 10 EUR
- Day trip to Monaco: train 4 EUR return (25 min) | Casino de Monte-Carlo entry 17 EUR
- Day trip to Cannes: train 6 EUR return (30 min)
- Day trip to Èze village: free to visit

Train within Riviera:
- Nice ↔ Monaco: 4 EUR (25 min)
- Nice ↔ Cannes: 6 EUR (35 min)

Recommended stay: 3-4 days

=== LYON ===
France's culinary capital. UNESCO-listed old town. Great base for Burgundy wine region.

Average daily costs:
- Budget: 55-70 EUR/day
- Mid-range: 100-150 EUR/day

Top experiences:
- Vieux-Lyon (UNESCO): FREE
- Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière: FREE
- Institut Lumière (cinema birthplace): 8 EUR
- Traboules (secret passages): FREE with guide
- Bouchon Lyonnais meal (traditional): 20-35 EUR/person

Recommended stay: 2 days
